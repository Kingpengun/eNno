Become A Teacher

STEMpals needs your help! We need highschoolers to help fulfil our mission of teaching underprivliged kids. All you need to do is create lessons and then teach them each week! In addtion to this the teachers earn some benfits:

  • Earn over 90 volunteer hours by the end of the year!
  • Virtual classes allow you to teach from anywhere easily and efficiently!
  • Prizes are avalible for the best teacher each month!

Join us to make an impact and improve the opportunites for students around the country!

Make a Chapter

We want to focus on the empowerment and growth of young minds in our program. Our class lessons are designed to make sure that your child fully learns the standards of their grade, and the standards for grades higher as well. By having co-teachers in each class, students would get to have the experience of learning in a group setting, but also getting individualized attention and teaching as well. We ensure to know every student by name and need, and we hope that you join our program!

  • Weekly classes with high quality materials and teachers
  • Free homework help sessions given with the purchase of classes for any topics!
  • All proceeds go to underprivliged schools in our area along with being donated to charities aimed at helping bridge the inequality gap.

Join us to make an impact and improve the opportunites for students around the country!
